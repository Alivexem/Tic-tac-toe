GAME MODES

HOW TO PLAY

The goal is straightforward, you choose between X and O, click the black boxes to input your choice alphabet (X plays first).

HOW TO WIN: Arrange your alphabets in 3 rows or columns side by side without having your opponent's alphabet in-between.

TWO PLAYER MODE: This will be done on the host device imitating the Traditional Tic Tac Toe played on paper. Players take turn to use host device.

VS COMPUTER MODE: This is a single player mode, you play against Computer.
